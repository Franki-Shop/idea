IdeaDeck

Lightweight web-app to generate idea deck with call to action buttons

Helping innovators, entrepreneurs, and non-profits clearly and concisely explain their idea and product.

There's beauty in Simplicity

  • Over 100 million businesses are launched annually.
  • Innovators need ways to convey their idea clearly to their audience.
  • IdeaDeck: Easily create, host and share your ideas with call to action buttons.
  • Works on all devices, ultra-light weight and doesn't require any javascript.
